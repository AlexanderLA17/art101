How to refill a tire. (instructions from ChatGPT)

Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. Find the Recommended Pressure

Look on the inside of your driver’s side door or check your owner’s manual. It’ll be listed in PSI (pounds per square inch).

Don’t use the number on the tire’s sidewall—that’s the max limit, not the recommended pressure.

2. Remove the Valve Cap

Unscrew the little cap on the tire’s air valve (looks like a small metal or plastic nozzle).

3. Check the Current Pressure

Press your tire pressure gauge onto the valve stem.

It might hiss briefly, but hold it firmly until you get a reading.

4. Add Air if Needed

If the pressure is too low, use an air compressor:

Plug it in (or use a coin-operated one at a gas station).

Attach the air hose to the valve stem—push it in snugly.

Squeeze the trigger (or press the lever, depending on the model).

Air will begin to flow. Add air in small bursts to avoid overinflating.

5. Recheck the Pressure

Use your gauge to measure again.

Keep adjusting until you hit the target PSI.

6. Replace the Valve Cap

Screw the valve cap back on so no dirt or moisture gets in.

7. Repeat for All Tires

Follow the same steps for the rest of the tires.

Pseudocode a Simple Game

Hangman:

Create a counter that will make you lose the game when 6 guesses have failed.

Create a word limit which should be longer or shorter depending on how difficult you want the game to be.

Create a random word generator API using something like Supabase or manually add words yourself.

Accept user input. (Restrict to letter guesses only)

Evaluate possible moves (can’t guess same letter twice)

Restrict letters not used in word or phrase.

Play with a friend and try to guess the phrase before them!
